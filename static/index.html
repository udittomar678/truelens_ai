// HOW IT WORKS
STEP 01
CNN Inference
EfficientNet-B3 analyses pixels and textures. A parallel FFT branch detects invisible frequency artefacts left by AI generators.
STEP 02
EXIF Forensics
Real photos contain camera metadata. AI images typically have none. Missing or suspicious EXIF is a strong signal.
STEP 03
Spectrum Analysis
GAN generators leave grid-like patterns in frequency space. Diffusion models show abnormal high-frequency energy.
STEP 04
Fusion Verdict
All signals are weighted and fused. CNN carries 65%, EXIF 20%, FFT 15%. Risk is classified LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH.
